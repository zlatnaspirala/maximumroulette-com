This project is free for use.

LINK TO 3D CHAT
Person 1 - Click Host and type on keyboard name of channel
and press ENTER .Send secret channel name to your friend (with email or any other communication site)
Person 2 - Click on host or join and type on keyboard name of same channel and press ENTER .

When you get connected , Use icon on top middle position to share your media...
